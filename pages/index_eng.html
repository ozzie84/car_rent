About us

Georgia may be called the 8 miracles of the world. Our company will help you cover all her beauty. Renting a car from the company Car Rent - means comfortable rest without problems. All our cars are insured and operating. We provide 24/7 technical support. You may take the car yourself or order a "car with a driver" service. The car will be delivered at any time to any point in Tbilisi and Batumi. Our fleet provides cars for all occasions.

If you order a Subaru Forester 4x4, then you know the abilities of this car that surpasses many off-road cars. Our company has cars and business class.

By taking advantage of the services of our company, you will only have to enjoy the hospitality of Georgia.

Rental conditions

All customers must accept the following conditions below

Who can rent

  • People 21 or over 21 years old.
  • Persons Owning an International Certificate of Driving License.
  • The tenant must present the valid driver's license and valid id card (international passport).

Who can drive

  • A rented car can only be operated by the tenant, it is forbidden to transfer the rented car for the management of any third party, or to unauthorized persons.
  • Renting a car is forbidden to drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

Operation of cars - renting and returning a car

  • The car can be picked up or left only in pre-agreed destinations.
  • The car can be picked up at Tbilisi International Airport or in any other place in Tbilisi.
  • If the tenant wants to install another destination in order to pick up or rent a car, it must be pre-agreed with the company's representatives.
  • The condition of the car should be clean, technically the same as before the rent. The fuel level in the gas tank must remain the same as before the lease.
  • The car must be returned strictly within 24 hours. In case of a delay of less than an hour, the company will levy a rent of 20% of the cost of a one-day car lease. If more than one hour but less than 3 hours, the lessor will be charged 50% of the daily rent, if more than 3 hours the lessor will be charged the full amount of rent in one day.
  • Mandatory deposit is $ 100. Taken as insurance if there are fines or other violations of the contract. Upon delivery of the car to the lender, if there are no violations, the pledge is returned.

Insurance

  • Vehicles are completely insured by a tenant's franchise.
  • In case of car damage, if the tenant cannot establish the cause of the breakdown (damage) (for example: the breakdown occurred when the car was parked and was not in the field of view of the tenant), in such case, the tenant's franchise will be $ 50.
  • In case of the car accident, the rented car should not move from the accident site.
  • In case of car accident, the lessee shall not release the other party involved in the accident.
  • The tenant must cooperate with the police and the company-owner of the car, otherwise the full cost of the damage will be retained from the tenant.
